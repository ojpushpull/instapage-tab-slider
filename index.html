Official member of the Jij justice league

By donating monthly, you will become an esteemed member of the JIJ Justice League, a distinguished tier of supporters who enjoy exclusive benefits and heightened attention from our organization. Stand with us as we champion justice and make a lasting impact on Israeli society.

Participate in Justice Forums

As a monthly donor, you will have the unique opportunity to engage in our quarterly Justice Forums. These webinars enable you to join live chat discussions, contribute your thoughts, and influence upcoming topics, ensuring that your voice is heard in our pursuit of justice.

Provide Help to At-Risk Communities

Your donations will directly support vulnerable groups in Israel, including prostitutes and sex trafficking victims, Holocaust survivors, and lone soldiers in the IDF. With your help, we can make a tangible difference in their lives, offering hope and assistance in times of need.

Advocate & Educate on Behalf of Israel

Empower yourself and others with the educational materials we provide, including newsletters, social media posts, and blog articles. Share these resources with friends and family to raise awareness and foster a deeper understanding of Israel's challenges and achievements.

Annual Affidavits & Quarterly Briefs

Stay informed about the impact of your support with exclusive access to quarterly and annual impact reports. These updates demonstrate how JIJ is making strides in promoting justice, thanks to your contributions.

Support our Free Legal Aid

Your monthly donations will directly fund the Jerusalem Institute of Justice's free legal aid, offering crucial assistance to individuals, families, and organizations in Israel. Together, we can make a difference in people's lives and uphold justice for all.
